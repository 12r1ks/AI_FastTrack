# CityPark — Parking Facility Information

## Overview

CityPark operates two secure parking facilities across Maplewood. Reservations are made exclusively online through the CityPark website. Both facilities operate 24/7.

- **Total capacity:** 20 spots across 2 locations
- **Reservations:** Online only, via the CityPark website
- **Access:** 24/7 at both locations

## Billing Rules (Both Locations)

- Hourly rate applies to bookings under 24 hours.
- Daily rate applies to bookings of 24 hours or more per day; it is cheaper than 10+ hourly hours.
- Payment is collected in full at the time of booking confirmation.
- No partial refunds for unused time.
- Multi-day bookings are charged as: number of full days × daily rate.

---

## Rules & Regulations

1. Reservations are required before arrival — walk-ins are not accepted.
2. Arrive within 15 minutes of your reservation start time; late arrivals may forfeit the spot.
3. The reserved spot may only be used by the vehicle specified at booking (by registration number).
4. The following are strictly prohibited on the premises:
   - Storage of flammable or hazardous materials
   - Vehicle repairs or maintenance on site
   - Overnight sleeping in vehicles
5. CityPark is not responsible for theft, damage, or loss of vehicles or belongings.
6. Vehicles parked without a valid reservation will be towed at the owner's expense.
7. Trucks and oversized vehicles are only permitted at CityPark East (Location 2).

## Booking Process

Reservations can only be made online through the CityPark website chat.

**Steps to reserve:**

1. Open the CityPark website chat assistant.
2. Provide your details: full name, surname, vehicle registration number, desired location, spot type, and reservation period (start and end date/time).
3. Submit the reservation request. An admin will review and approve or reject it.
4. You will receive a confirmation message with a QR code once approved.
5. On arrival, scan the QR code at the entrance gate to open it.

**Cancellations:**
- Cancellations made more than 1 hours before the reservation start time are eligible for a full refund.
- Cancellations after 1 hours before the reservation are non-refundable.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I book at either location?**
A: Yes. Choose the location that suits you when making a reservation. Trucks must use CityPark East.

**Q: Can I extend my reservation while already parked?**
A: Yes, if the spot is not reserved by another user after your booking, you can extend online through the website chat.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard). Payment is processed online at time of booking.

**Q: What happens if I arrive late?**
A: You have a 15-minute grace period. After that, the spot may be released. Contact support immediately if delayed.

**Q: Can I book for someone else?**
A: Yes, but you must provide the actual vehicle registration number that will use the spot.

**Q: Is the facility monitored?**
A: Yes, CCTV cameras operate 24/7 at both locations. Footage is retained for 30 days.

**Q: What if the entrance gate does not open?**
A: Use the intercom at the gate or contact support through the website. Support is available 24/7.
