# CityPark — Parking Facility Information

## Overview

CityPark is a small, secure parking facility located in the heart of Maplewood. It offers 3 individually numbered parking spots available for reservation through the CityPark website. The facility operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

- **Total capacity:** 3 spots
- **Location:** Maplewood city centre
- **Access:** 24/7
- **Reservations:** Online only, via the CityPark website

---

## Location & Directions

**Address:** 14 Elm Street, Maplewood, 10001

The facility is located one block east of Maplewood Central Station. Look for the green CityPark sign above the entrance gate on Elm Street.

**Landmarks:**
- 100 m from Maplewood Central Station (east exit)
- Opposite the Elm Street post office
- Next to the Sunrise Café

**Entrance:** Vehicle gate on Elm Street — opens automatically when a valid reservation QR code is scanned.

---

## Parking Spots

The facility has 3 spots across two categories:

| Spot | Type | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| A1 | Standard (outdoor) | Open-air spot, suitable for standard-size vehicles |
| A2 | Standard (outdoor) | Open-air spot, suitable for standard-size vehicles |
| B1 | Premium (covered) | Covered spot near the entrance, suitable for all vehicle sizes |

All spots are suitable for cars and small vans. Motorcycles and bicycles are not permitted.

---

## Pricing

Prices are per spot per booking.

| Spot Type | Hourly Rate | Daily Rate (up to 24 h) |
|-----------|-------------|--------------------------|
| Standard (A1, A2) | €2.50 / hour | €15.00 / day |
| Premium covered (B1) | €4.00 / hour | €22.00 / day |

**Billing rules:**
- Hourly rate applies to bookings under 24 hours.
- Daily rate applies to bookings of exactly 24 hours; it is cheaper than 10+ hourly hours for standard spots.
- Payment is collected in full at the time of booking confirmation.
- No partial refunds for unused time.

## Rules & Regulations

1. Reservations are required before arrival — walk-ins are not accepted.
2. Arrive within 15 minutes of your reservation start time; late arrivals may forfeit the spot.
3. The reserved spot may only be used by the vehicle specified at booking (by car registration number).
4. The following are strictly prohibited on the premises:
   - Storage of flammable or hazardous materials
   - Vehicle repairs or maintenance
   - Overnight sleeping in vehicles
5. CityPark is not responsible for theft, damage, or loss of vehicles or belongings.
6. Vehicles parked without a valid reservation will be towed at the owner's expense.
7. Maximum vehicle height: 2.1 m (covered spot B1 only).

---

## Booking Process

Reservations can only be made online through the CityPark website. There is no phone or walk-in booking.

**Steps to reserve:**

1. Visit the CityPark website and open the chat or reservation form.
2. Provide your details: full name, surname, car registration number, desired spot, and reservation period (start date/time and end date/time).
3. Submit the reservation request. An admin will review and approve or reject it.
4. You will receive a confirmation message with a QR code once approved.
5. On arrival, scan the QR code at the entrance gate to open it.

**Cancellations:**
- Cancellations made more than 2 hours before the reservation start time are eligible for a full refund.
- Cancellations within 2 hours of the start time are non-refundable.
- To cancel, use the cancellation link in your confirmation email or contact support via the website.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I extend my reservation while I am already parked?**
A: Yes, if the spot is not reserved by another user after your current booking, you can extend online through the website.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) are accepted. Payment is processed online at the time of booking.

**Q: What happens if I arrive late?**
A: You have a 15-minute grace period. After that, the spot may be released. Contact support immediately if you are delayed.

**Q: Can I book for someone else?**
A: Yes, but you must provide the actual vehicle's registration number that will use the spot.

**Q: Is the facility monitored?**
A: Yes, CCTV cameras operate 24/7. Footage is retained for 30 days.

**Q: What if the entrance gate does not open?**
A: Use the intercom at the gate or contact support through the website. Support is available 24/7.
